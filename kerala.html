Kovalam Beach: For Friends & Family

Kovalam is a beachside, quaint little town near Thiruvananthapuram. This town has three beaches, separated by rocks. The Lighthouse beach is one of the most romantic beaches in Kerala and is considered as the paradise of the south. Things to do: Check out the three beaches at Kovalam i.e. Lighthouse beach, Kovalam beach and Hawa Beach, Also see the Neyyar Dam Places to eat: 3rd Rock, Bait, Oyster Restaurant, Curry Leaf, Varsha restaurant, Malabar Cafe Popular beach resorts: The Leela, Golden Sands, Royal sea Blue, Turtle on the beach

Kappad Beach: Beach With Historical Importance

Kappad has interesting history attached to it. The explorer Vasco Da Gama landed on the shores of the town Calicut now known as Kozhikode, when he docked in India in the year 1498. Another romantic getaway, this beach is serene and not too overwhelming which makes it one of the best beaches in Kerala. Things to do: Check out the thusharagiri falls, janaki forest Places to eat: M-grill, White House, Hotel Paragon Popular beach resorts: Kappad beach resort, WelcomHotel Raviz Kadavu Resort, Marina Residency
