Manori – A Peaceful Nirvana At A Stone Throw

One of the best beaches near Mumbai, Manori is also known as ‘Mini Goa’ for its natural landscape and activities. Located near the village and creek of the same name, the sparkling white sand Manori Beach, encircled by casurina plantations is a fabulous option for a quick and refreshing day outing away from the city noises. Alibaug – For Peace Loving Travellers

The search for good beaches near Mumbai ends with the coastal town of Alibaug, located in the centre of Konkan region. Azure blue sea, black sand beaches, and buzzing shores with beach shacks make Alibaug an all-time favorite option in the league of beach destinations near Mumbai. Best Time to Visit: November to March Major attractions: Ferry ride to Kulaba Fort, kite surfing, paragliding, and kayaking
