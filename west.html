Sagar Beach - ideal for Families, friends, and couple

Gangasagar is known as Sagar Island and it is one of the most visited beaches near Kolkata. From nature lovers to religious people, this island has something for each type of traveler. If you are a beach lover then Sagar Beach located in Sagar Island will fulfill all your dreams about a laid-back vacation. From savoring the view of the sunset to taking a relaxing dip in the crystal clear beach, there is a lot you can do at Sagar beach. Gangasagar is the nearest beach to Kolkata. You can also attend the famous Gangasagar Mela which is celebrated in a grand way every year. Things To Do In Sagar Beach: Beach walking, attend the Mela, Sunbathing Ideal For: Families, friends, and couple Ideal Trip Duration: 1-2 days Weather: 20 °C – 41 °C Best Time To Visit Sagar Beach: October-January How To Reach: One can reach Sagar Beach from Kolkata by road in 3-4 hours. Take a flight to Kolkata and from there take a cab to reach the place Distance: 120 Km from Kolkata Stay Options: Beach hotels and resorts nearby are the best options for staying

Bakkhali Beach - ideal for Families, friends, and couple

If you are planning a quiet holiday with your loved ones, then Bakkhali Beach is a must stopover. One of the most crowded beaches along the coastline of West-Bengal, the seashore is thronged with tourists and locals. Apart from the sapphire blue waters and gushing sound of the waves, the beach has a lot of other attractions too. For instance, when not in a mood to take a stroll along the coast then one can pay a visit to the nearby Bhagabatpur Crocodile project or indulge in a boat ride towards the Jambudweep Island where mystic forests await to take you in their laps. Things To Do In Bakkhali Beach: Beach walking, Island hopping Ideal For: Families, friends, and couples Ideal Trip Duration: 1-2 days Weather: 20 °C-41 °C Best Time To Visit Bakkhali Beach: October-March. How To Reach: One can reach Bakkhali beach from Kolkata by road in 4-5 hours. Take a flight to Kolkata and from there take a cab to reach the place Distance: 130 Km from Kolkata Stay Options: Beach hotels and resorts nearby are the best options for staying
